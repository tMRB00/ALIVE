West Philadelphia Community Survey Barnes West – a collaboration between The Barnes Foundation and People’s Emergency Center Community Development Corporation – administered a community survey in the early months of 2021. The primary goal was to find out how West Philadelphia residents found out about special events and important information in their area, as a means of better informing Barnes West outreach efforts for artistic programming.
